Come in to Chill & Relax In Rest-o

Welcome Mabuhay and welcome to Direda Flowed—your go-to spot for authentic Filipino cuisine served in a warm, casual setting. We’re proud to share the rich flavors and traditions of the Philippines through beloved dishes like adobo, sinigang, kare-kare, and lechon kawali. Each meal is prepared with love, using time-honored recipes and the freshest ingredients. Whether you're here to relive comforting memories of home or discover something new, we invite you to relax, enjoy, and savor every bite. Experience the warmth of Filipino hospitality—right here at Direda Flowed.
